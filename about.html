Who is Sangoma Wamandlozi?

Ancestral Connection & Divination

Through bone reading and spiritual insight, we help individuals receive messages from their ancestors, guiding them toward clarity and purpose.

Healing & Protection Rituals

Using powerful African herbs, spiritual ceremonies, and cleansing rituals, we remove negativity, strengthen relationships, and attract prosperity.

At Sangoma Wamandlozi, we honor the ancient wisdom of African spiritual healing, guided by ancestral spirits and sacred traditions. With deep-rooted knowledge in divination, herbal healing (muthi), and spiritual cleansing, we provide holistic solutions for those seeking balance, protection, and guidance in their lives. Our mission is to reconnect individuals with their ancestral path and restore harmony between the spiritual and physical worlds.At Sangoma Wamandlozi, tradition meets spiritual wisdom to bring healing, transformation, and ancestral guidance.
